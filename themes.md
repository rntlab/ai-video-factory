### Monday（月曜）— スキンケア覚醒：疲れた朝から輝きへ

**テーマ:** 朝の疲労感から肌が蘇る瞬間の感情変化
**ターゲット商品:** グロウ系美容液 / レチノールセラム
**動画意図:** 「疲労→驚き→静かな自信」の感情弧。before/afterを光の質感変化で表現し衝動買いを促す。

**プロンプト:**
```
[0-3s]: close-up, a young anime woman, delicate tired eyes, dark under-circles, sits at vanity mirror in blue pre-dawn light, she stares at her reflection with quiet resignation, static locked shot, cool dim lighting
[3-7s]: slow push-in, she applies a glowing serum drop to her cheek, eyes flutter closed, a warm luminous ripple spreads outward from the touch point, lighting shifts from cold blue to warm amber
[7-10s]: medium shot, she opens eyes slowly, skin visibly transformed and radiant, lips part in quiet disbelief then soft wonder, one sakura petal drifts past the window, camera holds still, golden rim light separating her from the soft-focus background
avoid jitter, avoid bent limbs, avoid temporal flicker, maintain face consistency, sharp clarity, stable picture. Theatrical-quality anime style, high-quality movie frame.
```
